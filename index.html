Dvar Torah of the Week

Parshat Bereshit: Beginning with Purpose

In the beginning, G-d created the heavens and the earth. The Torah starts not with laws or commandments, but with creation itself—teaching us that everything has a purpose and design.

As teenagers, we often question our purpose and place in the world. The creation story reminds us that just as each element of creation had its specific role, we too were created with intention and meaning.

The Midrash teaches that G-d created and destroyed many worlds before settling on this one. Perhaps this is a metaphor for our own journey—we try, fail, and begin again until we find our true path.

Source: Rabbi Jonathan Sacks, "Covenant & Conversation"
